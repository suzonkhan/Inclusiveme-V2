About Us

Welcome to Inclusive Me

At Inclusive Me, we’re more than just a news platform; we’re a bridge that connects people, cultures, and stories. Whether you’re seeking the latest updates, insights on international trends, or a window into global lifestyles, we’re here to keep you informed and inspired—all in the language you’re most comfortable with.

Our Story

Inclusive Me was born out of a simple but powerful idea: information should unite, not divide. Our founders recognized that many immigrants in Italy face barriers to staying informed, whether due to language differences or a lack of relatable content. Inspired by the vibrant, diverse voices of immigrant communities, Inclusive Me launched as a digital journal with one mission: to make news accessible and meaningful to everyone.

We started by curating articles in multiple languages, ensuring that everyone can access reliable information—whether it’s global politics, local updates, or cultural insights. Today, we continue to grow with our audience, striving to provide content that educates, empowers, and builds connections.

Our Mission

To break down language barriers and foster understanding by delivering reliable, multilingual news content tailored to the diverse communities in Italy.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and inclusive digital news platform in Italy, where individuals from all backgrounds feel represented, informed, and empowered to engage with the world around them.

What we stand for

Inclusivity

We embrace diversity in every sense—cultures, languages, and perspectives. At Inclusive Me, everyone’s story matters.

Reliability

Our commitment is to provide accurate, unbiased news sourced from reputable outlets, so you can trust what you read.

Accessibility

From content in multiple languages to user-friendly designs, we aim to make news reachable for everyone, no matter where they are or what language they speak.

Connection

We believe news is a tool to bring people closer together. By sharing stories across borders, we foster empathy and understanding.

What we offer

Multilingual News

Our articles are available in Italian, Arabic, French, Spanish, Bangla, Urdu, English, and more—so you can stay informed in the language that feels like home.

Diverse Content

From breaking news to lifestyle, economy, and politics, we cover a broad range of topics to keep you updated on what matters most.

Community Engagement

Our social media platforms are designed to engage, inspire, and involve our audience. Follow us on TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook for dynamic, human-centered content that brings stories to life.

Why Inclusive Me?

We’re not just another news platform. We’re a community-driven initiative with a heart for inclusivity and a passion for storytelling. By choosing Inclusive Me, you’re choosing to be part of a movement that values diversity and the power of knowledge.

Meet Our Team

Behind Inclusive Me is a dedicated group of digital experts from diverse backgrounds. We’re united by our belief that information is a right, not a privilege, and by our commitment to building bridges between communities.

Let’s Stay Connected

At Inclusive Me, your voice matters. Whether you have feedback, a story to share, or just want to say hello, we’re here to listen. Together, we can make this platform even better.
